## Sample Prescription Notes

### For Lower Back Pain:
```
Diagnosis: Mechanical lower back pain

Treatment Plan:
- Manual therapy - 3 sessions per week
- Core strengthening exercises
- Postural correction

Home Exercise Program:
1. Pelvic tilts - 10 reps, 3 sets daily
2. Bridge exercise - 10 reps, 3 sets daily
3. Cat-cow stretch - 10 reps, 2 times daily

Precautions:
- Avoid prolonged sitting
- Maintain proper posture
- No heavy lifting

Follow-up: After 1 week
```

### For Shoulder Pain:
```
Diagnosis: Rotator cuff strain

Treatment Plan:
- Manual therapy and mobilization
- Strengthening exercises
- Electrotherapy for pain relief

Home Exercise Program:
1. Pendulum exercises - 10 reps, 3 times daily
2. Shoulder external rotation - 10 reps, 3 sets
3. Scapular squeeze - Hold 5 sec, 10 reps

Precautions:
- Avoid overhead activities
- Apply ice after exercises
- Gradual progression

Follow-up: After 3 days
```

### For Knee Pain:

Diagnosis: Patellofemoral pain syndrome

2. Wall squats - Hold 10 sec, 10 reps

Follow-up: After 5 days
